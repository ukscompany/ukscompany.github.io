EbookMoa Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-04-11

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the EbookMoa mobile application operated by UksCompany. It explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect personal data in connection with EbookMoa.

If you are looking for the general UksCompany privacy page for other services, please see /privacy.html.

If you have privacy-related questions, contact us at ukscompany24@gmail.com.

1. Operator Information

Operator: UksCompany

Contact Email: ukscompany24@gmail.com

2. How We Use Personal Data

We may process personal data for the following purposes:

  • to provide and operate the EbookMoa app
  • to authenticate users and maintain account sessions
  • to sync favorite books across devices
  • to respond to issue reports and support inquiries
  • to improve service quality, reliability, and security
  • to measure app usage and performance
  • to display advertising and measure advertising-related performance
  • to comply with legal obligations

3. Categories Of Data We May Process

Depending on how you use EbookMoa, we may process:

  • account identifiers
  • nickname and account-related timestamps
  • favorite-book identifiers and sync metadata
  • book issue reports submitted by users
  • device and operating system information
  • IP address and network-related information
  • usage events and screen-view information
  • advertising identifiers or similar identifiers
  • locally stored preference data

4. Login And Account Management

EbookMoa supports sign-in using Firebase Authentication and third-party identity providers such as Google, Kakao, and Apple.

For account operation, we may process:

  • Firebase UID
  • nickname
  • account-related timestamps

At this time, the EbookMoa server does not intentionally store your email address, display name, or profile photo in its own database for routine account operation. However, Firebase and your selected identity provider may process such information during authentication.

5. Favorite Books Synchronization

If you are logged in, EbookMoa may sync your favorite books with our server so that your data can be restored across devices or after reinstalling the app.

We may process:

  • favorite-book identifiers
  • internal favorite IDs
  • related synchronization timestamps

If you are not logged in, some favorite-book data may remain stored locally on your device.

6. Book Issue Reports

If you submit a book issue report through EbookMoa, we may process:

  • book ID
  • ISBN
  • title
  • author
  • publisher
  • publication date
  • report reason
  • report details
  • your user ID and nickname, if you are logged in

We use this information to review reports and improve service quality.

7. Analytics, Advertising, And Device-Related Processing

EbookMoa may use analytics and advertising tools such as Firebase Analytics, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), and Google Play Services.

These tools may process:

  • app usage events
  • screen-view data
  • device and operating system information
  • advertising identifiers or similar data
  • network-related information

On iOS, the app may request tracking permission where required by the platform.

8. On-Device Storage

EbookMoa may store certain data locally on your device, including:

  • offline favorites
  • cached favorite-book lists for logged-in users
  • review-prompt timing information
  • login-gate display preferences
  • theme and app preference settings

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate EbookMoa, including for authentication, analytics, advertising, hosting, infrastructure, and platform-related features.

Examples may include:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Sign-In
  • Kakao login services
  • Sign in with Apple

These providers may process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google / Firebase
  • Kakao
  • Apple

10. Data Sharing, International Transfers, And Retention

We do not sell personal data.

We may disclose personal data:

  • to service providers that help us operate EbookMoa
  • to authentication providers when you choose a third-party login method
  • when required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • when necessary to protect rights, safety, security, or prevent fraud or abuse

Some third-party services we use may process data on servers located outside your country of residence. As a result, personal data may be transferred internationally where necessary for the operation of EbookMoa.

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the service, fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data involved.

11. Your Rights, Deletion Requests, And Children

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • request access to your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • request deletion of your personal data
  • request restriction of certain processing
  • object to certain processing
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To make a privacy-related request or deletion request, contact us at ukscompany24@gmail.com.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law. If we become aware that such data has been collected in violation of applicable law, we will take appropriate steps to delete or disable it.

12. Security And Changes To This Policy

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we may provide notice through the app, website, or other appropriate means. The updated version becomes effective as of the date shown at the top of this page.
