Planet Tactics Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Planet Tactics mobile game (the “Application”), operated by UksCompany (욱스컴퍼니) (the “Service Provider”). It explains what information is processed when you play the game, view ads, enable notifications, or make an in-app purchase.

Planet Tactics does not require a UksCompany account. The Service Provider does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or precise location to play the game.

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact ukscompany24@gmail.com.

1. Operator Information

Application: Planet Tactics

Operator: UksCompany (욱스컴퍼니)

Privacy Contact: ukscompany24@gmail.com

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The Application stores game progress and preferences in its private local storage so that you can continue playing. This information may include:

  • current and completed stages, failure counts, and tutorial progress;
  • virtual-currency balances, upgrades, owned items, item loadouts, and unlocked slots;
  • energy status and timestamps used to calculate energy recovery;
  • rewarded-ad counters used to apply daily limits and calculate in-game rewards;
  • purchase tokens already applied on the device, used to prevent a purchase from being granted twice;
  • language, game speed, sound, music, haptic-feedback, notification, and other gameplay preferences.

This locally stored game data is not a cloud save and is not linked to a UksCompany account. It may be lost if you clear the Application's data or uninstall the Application. On supported upgrades, the Application may read locally stored progress from an earlier version of Planet Tactics to migrate that progress on the same device.

3. Google Analytics for Firebase

Release versions of the Application use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the game is used and to improve gameplay, reliability, and the in-game economy. The SDK may automatically process an app-instance identifier, IP address, approximate region, device and operating-system information, app version, language, sessions, app launches, screen views, and engagement information.

The Application also records limited gameplay events concerning:

  • stage starts, completions, failures, and exits;
  • the highest stage completed;
  • virtual currency earned or spent, upgrades, item usage, and slot unlocks;
  • rewarded-ad completion and the related reward placement;
  • in-app purchase flow starts and verified virtual-currency grants;
  • screen or tab views and language changes.

The Application does not send your locally saved game file or a UksCompany account identifier to Firebase Analytics. Analytics collection is disabled in ordinary debug and profile builds used for development.

4. Advertising, Consent, and Tracking Choices

Planet Tactics displays banner and rewarded ads provided by Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as IP address, general location inferred from IP address, advertising or other device identifiers, app and ad interactions, and diagnostic or performance information. Google may use this information to provide and measure ads, perform analytics, and prevent fraud and abuse.

The Application uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and record advertising privacy choices where required. Depending on your region and previous choices, a privacy-options entry may be available in the Application's settings so you can review or change those choices. A decision not to allow personalized advertising does not necessarily remove ads; non-personalized or limited ads may still be displayed where permitted.

On iOS, the Application may ask for permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before accessing the advertising identifier for tracking. You may deny that request or later manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. On Android, you may reset or delete the advertising ID through device settings where supported.

5. In-App Purchases

Where available, Planet Tactics offers consumable virtual-currency packs through Google Play or Apple's App Store. Google or Apple processes the payment method and payment transaction. UksCompany does not receive or store your full payment-card or bank-account details.

To provide the purchased content and prevent duplicate or fraudulent grants, the Application and the Service Provider's purchase server may process:

  • store platform and product identifier;
  • purchase token, transaction identifier, or receipt data;
  • purchase status and store order identifier, where provided;
  • the amount of virtual currency granted;
  • store verification responses and verification timestamps.

Purchase-verification records are not associated with a UksCompany user account. The Application may also keep a limited list of applied purchase tokens on the device to avoid granting the same purchase more than once.

6. Application Server and Network Requests

The Application may communicate with the Planet Tactics server to retrieve the active in-app product catalog, verify purchases, and receive service notices or update information. Like most internet services, the server receives technical request information such as IP address, request time, requested path, and request status. Requests for notices or updates may also include the app version, build number, platform, language, app name, and package identifier.

To reduce manipulation of device time when calculating energy, the Application may request only the standard network time response from Google, Cloudflare, or Apple. Those providers receive standard network information, including your IP address, when the request is made.

7. Local Notifications

If you enable notifications in the Application's settings, Planet Tactics may schedule an on-device reminder for when your play energy is full. Notification permission is requested only after you choose to enable this feature. These reminders are scheduled locally; the Application does not register a push-notification token with UksCompany's server for this feature. You can disable reminders in the Application or operating-system settings.

8. Third-Party Services

The Application uses or communicates with the following providers:

  • Google Privacy Policy — Google Play, Google Analytics for Firebase, Google AdMob, advertising consent, purchase processing, and network time;
  • Privacy and Security in Firebase — Google Analytics for Firebase;
  • Google Advertising Policies — Google AdMob and advertising technologies;
  • Apple Privacy Policy — App Store purchases, platform permissions, and network time;
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy — network time requests.

These providers process information under their own privacy policies and may process it in countries other than your country of residence. UksCompany does not sell personal data. Information may be disclosed where required by applicable law, legal process, or a valid governmental request, or where reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, security, and prevent fraud or abuse.

9. Retention and Deletion

  • Local game progress and preferences remain on the device until replaced, cleared, or removed when the Application is uninstalled.
  • Purchase-verification records may be retained as needed to deliver purchases, prevent duplicate grants and fraud, handle refunds and disputes, and satisfy accounting or legal obligations.
  • Server access and security logs are retained only as reasonably necessary for operation, troubleshooting, security, and abuse prevention.
  • Information processed by Google, Apple, or Cloudflare is retained and deleted according to the applicable provider's policies and service settings.

Because Planet Tactics does not provide a UksCompany account, local game progress cannot be identified or remotely deleted by UksCompany. You can delete local progress by clearing the Application's data or uninstalling it. To request access to or deletion of data handled directly by the Service Provider, contact ukscompany24@gmail.com. We may need transaction details to locate a purchase record, and some records may need to be retained where required by law or for fraud prevention.

10. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on your device, region, and applicable law, you may:

  • review or change advertising consent through the privacy options shown in the Application;
  • manage tracking and advertising identifiers in device settings;
  • enable or disable local notification permission;
  • clear locally stored game data or uninstall the Application;
  • request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection concerning personal data handled by the Service Provider;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

These rights may be subject to exceptions under applicable law. To submit a request, email ukscompany24@gmail.com.

11. Children's Privacy

Planet Tactics is not directed to children under 13, and UksCompany does not knowingly request or collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information to UksCompany, contact ukscompany24@gmail.com so that appropriate action can be taken. Age thresholds and parental consent requirements may differ by country.

12. Security

The Service Provider uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. Communications with the Planet Tactics production server use HTTPS, and third-party SDK data is protected in transit according to the applicable provider's practices. However, no transmission or storage method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the Application, third-party services, legal requirements, or our data practices. We will publish the revised policy on this page and update the effective date. Where required, we will provide additional notice or request consent through the Application.

14. Contact Us

For questions or requests concerning this Privacy Policy or Planet Tactics' privacy practices, contact UksCompany at ukscompany24@gmail.com.
